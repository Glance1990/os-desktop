JavaScriptOpen
JavaScript is a programing language used extensively in the creation of websites.

How to update settings
Warning: disabling JavaScript can interfere with the functionality of most modern websites.

Chrome
Open the 3 dot menu located in the top right of your browser
Click Settings
Scroll to the bottom and click Advanced
Click Site Settings
Click JavaScript
Update your settings
