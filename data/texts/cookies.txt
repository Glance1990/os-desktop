Cookies are small files which are stored on your computer. They are used to hold data specific to a particular browser and website, and can be accessed either by the web server or your computer. They are also sadly inedible. 

Clear or Delete Existing Cookies
Chrome
Go to Settings
Scroll to bottom and click Advanced
Scroll to Privacy and Security
Click Clear Browsing Data
In the modal check Cookies and other site data
Click the Clear data button
